Digital data collection is hard.

In order to do data collection right, we need to understand what makes doing it well so hard.
Multiple platforms and channels.
To understand your users, you need to identify and track them across all the platforms and channels you engage with them, consolidate that data and build a single customer view.
Digital engagement varies widely.
Users don't engage with email, dating services or an banking apps in the same way. So we should not expect the data from each to look alike, or even be structured alike.
Data collection needs to constantly evolve.
Digital products and services are constantly evolving, and as they do, data collection to evolve with it. As we bulid understanding, the questions we ask of data are constantly evolving, and with it, so must our data collection.
