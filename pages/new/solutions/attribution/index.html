Attribution done Right

Doing attribution right is an essential in spending marketing budget effectively

But doing it right is hard.

  • Users may engage with multiple channels prior to converting.
  • It can be hard to trace an anonymous user across different platforms and channels to correctly identify all the advertising they have seen prior to converting
  • Valueing conversions is not straightforward: a user might have a value significantly higher or lower lifetime value than any initial transaction value.
  • There is no definitive way to measure the value generated by those channels.

Take a different approach to Attribution with Snowplow Insights

  • Decouple your data collection (what marketing touches each user engaged with and the revenue events for each user) with the logic for assigning value to marketing channels
  • Generate your own complete data set that details all the marketing touches and value generated from every one of your users and potential users, across all channels
  • Identify and apply the most reliable way to identify known and anonymous users across platforms and channels for your users, channels and business
  • Apply multiple attribution models to your data, and figure out what model or combination of models are best placed to enable you to optimize your marketing spend across each channel and campaign
  • First touch, last touch, linear, last non-direct, bath tub and time decay models delivered out-of-the-box
  • Extend with your own algorthmic or other models

Deliver meaningful results

  • Reliably attribute marketing spend and optimize spend allocation in a way that works best for your business, including your monetization model and real customer lifetime value
  • Own your own attribution data and logic: do not rely on a vendor to tell you the return on your marketing spend or where you should put your budget
  • Have full auditability of your ad spend performance calculations
