List of Sub-Processors

Last modified: May 2018

Snowplow Analytics Limited and Snowplow Analytics Inc. use certain sub-processors to assist it

  • Deliver Snowplow Insights to existing customers.
  • Develop leads, opportunities and ultimately sales with prospective customers.

Snowplow Insights customers and prospective customers interested in learning more about our approach to customer data protection should consult our Data Protection Agreement and get in touch with their Customer Success Manager. Prospective customers interested in how we use data e.g. collected from our website to manage our sales and marketing process should consult our privacy policy.
