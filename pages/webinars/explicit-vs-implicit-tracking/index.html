LIVE DEBATE

Explicit vs implicit tracking

Join Heap, Snowplow and Iteratively for a debate on the pros and cons of explicit and implicit tracking, when to use and why.

  • Paul Boocock

    Software Engineer at Snowplow

  • Patrick Thompson

    Co-founder and CEO at Iteratively

  • Dan Li

    Software Engineer at Heap

When it comes to data collection, should you opt for explicit or implicit tracking?

The debate that’s driven passionate discourse across the data world will now be the spotlight of our next webinar.


To give data teams and data consumers the full picture Snowplow, Heap and Iteratively have joined forces to discuss this topic in detail, going deep into the pros and cons of each approach, when to use them and why. It will be a debate style format, tapping into Heap’s expertise around implicit tracking and Snowplow’s expertise around explicit tracking. We’ll leave plenty of time for questions, so come prepared.


In the explicit corner is Paul, Software Engineer at Snowplow. He’ll be arguing in favour of explicit event capture.


And in the implicit corner is Dan Li, Software Engineer at Heap. He will argue the case of implicit event capture.

In this discussion, our speakers will debate:

  • The differences between implicit and explicit tracking and the philosophy behind them,
  • Some of the strengths and limitations of each approach,
  • Who, when and where it makes sense to use explicit over implicit tracking and vice versa.
