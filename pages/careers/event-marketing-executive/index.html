Event Marketing Executive

London

At Snowplow, we build technology that gives our users control of their digital event data, so they can use data to do incredible things.

To date, Snowplow has been used primarily by a group of pioneering, fast-growing, technically-sophisticated and data-savvy customers. As we move into the next phase of our growth, we need to get better at explaining to more traditional companies, across a range of geographies and sectors, what opportunities there for them to use data to differentiate and how we can work with them to support them realizing those opportunities.

Events play a key role in our marketing strategy:

  • We organise our own events and meetups across a large number of cities globally.
  • We sponsor, speak at and participate in meetups and events organised by other companies including partners and industry associations.

We are looking for an individual to join our marketing team to develop and execute our event management strategy.

Responsibilities

You will be responsible for:

  • Event organisation and delivery. We would look to you to identify opportunities to hold or participate in events, to organise and deliver events, and then measure their impact and assess whether we should participate in and/or organise similar events going forwards. This would involve working collaboratively with our internal team, users and partners, to identify speakers, organise material, market the events, identify suitable spaces for the events, hire videographers etc.
  • Work collaboratively with the rest of our growth team (which encompasses marketing, sales and customer success) to drive growth at Snowplow. We want to systematically target specific verticals and use cases. You would play an active role as part of that team, informing the strategic direction of that team and ensuring we the work you do on the events and digital marketing side support that overall strategy.

What we’re looking for

We’d love to get to know you if you are:

  • Focused on delivering and good at getting things done.
  • Very organised.
  • Passionate about data and technology and the transformational potential of data.
  • Excited to work in a young and fast-growing startup.
  • Want to work for a fast-growing startup.
  • Independent and able to self-manage.
  • A team player.

The following are a bonus but not necessarily required:

  • Experience in event management.
  • Experience working in technology and/or data.

Interested? Send your CV to recruitment@snowplowanalytics.com.
