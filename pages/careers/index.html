Join our team and empower a generation of Smart People to Differentiate with Data.

At Snowplow, we believe in the transformative potential of data. Data drives insight and insight drives opportunity.

Using data effectively is a creative as well as technical exercise: it starts with asking the right questions. Smart People at the heart of every successful data program. Unfortunately at too many companies, Smart People get tied up doing boring data-related tasks: wrestling data out of silo'd systems, ingesting it into warehouses and analytics engines and joining it with other data sets so that useful things can then be done with it.

We build technology that gives Smart People control of their own data. So they can focus on what Smart People are best at: being creative, asking the right question, choosing the analysis technique suitable for answering the question, running the analysis, interpreting the results and acting on those results.

Come join our team and help empower the next generation of Smart Digital analysts, scientists, engineers, marketers, product managers and more differentiate with data.
