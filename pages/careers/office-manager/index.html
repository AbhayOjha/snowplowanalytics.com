Office Manager

London

We are a fast growing startup, headquartered in London. Whilst the majority of our team is based in London, we have a large number of team members based remotely around the world.

Twelve months ago there were only five people based in our London office. There are now 12 and we are expanding rapidly.

We are looking for an Office Manager to:

  • Look after our team based in London.
  • Organise our twice yearly company away-weeks.
  • Ensure that the expansion of our team runs smoothly.

Responsibilities

You will be responsible for:

  • Keeping our team happy! We want to be a place that our team members love to work. We’re open to creative ideas to surprise and delighting our team members...
  • Ensuring our office is a pleasant environment where team members can work productively.
  • Ensure new joiners are well looked after. (E.g. receive a team welcome back, have a laptop and everything they need to get started.)

What we’re looking for

We’d love to get to know you if you are:

  • Focused on delivering and good at getting things done.
  • Very organised.
  • Highly personable and good at developing relationships.

The following are a bonus but not necessarily required:

  • Experience as an office manager.

Interested? Send your CV to recruitment@snowplowanalytics.com.

We do not welcome calls from recruitment consultants.
