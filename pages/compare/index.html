10 Reasons Why You Should Use Snowplow Insights

1 - Define Your Own Custom Events & Entities

No two businesses are the same and as a result, the events and entities they describe should be completely different. An online publisher might be interested in their most viral headlines whilst a streaming platform might want to understand who their most engaging influencers are. Unlike our competitors, who impose a rigid analytics structure on businesses from various industries, Snowplow Insights allows you to define your own events and entities from the ground. Choosing this paths means that you have to decide:

  1. The questions you want to answer.
  2. The data you want to track.
  3. How you want to use the data to answer those questions.
Whilst this means that Snowplow Insights takes longer to set up than our competitors, the long run benefit in answering the most important questions to your business is huge.

2 - 24 Hour Support

With our support contracts we offer 24-hour coverage to help our users effectively use our technology – this also includes workshops.

3 - Own Your Data

Wirth Snowplow Insights, your data lives in your own data warehouse – giving you full control over your data.

4 - Access To True Event Level Data

Our competitors apply their own conventions and processing to event-level data. Snowplow Insights gives you access to the full event stream which in turn allows our users to develop their own understanding of the various sequences of events.

5 - Low Latency

Snowplow users can run their pipelines hourly, loading data into Redshift with a 2 hour latency. Users of the Snowplow real-time pipeline have the full event stream available to consume in Kinesis and Elastic in seconds.

6 - Custom Enrichments

Snowplow Insights users can customise how their data is enriched which allows you to choose what data lands in your warehouse bottom-up. Check out the full list of our enrichments here.

7 - Data Modeling

With our unique approach to data modelling, you can evolve the way that you make sense of the raw, unopinionated, sequences of data over time.

8 - Out-of-the-box fields

There are several fields which are available in Snowplow upon setup which just aren’t supplied by several of our competitors:

  • IP Adresses of the end user.
  • Page ping i.e heartbeat events. These include a number of data points which allow you to calculate metrics such as how far down a page a user has scrolled (which in turn allows you to calculate how much content is read) as well as how long users spend on particular content items.
  • Performance timing data i.e data on how fast pages take to load.
  • IDFA (Open, Apple and Android) / IDFV (Apple).
Look at the full list of out-of-the-box table definitions here.
