  • Snowplow collects almost 100 unique data points per event - giving you multiple lenses and angles to query that data by
  • It’s guaranteed to be high quality - Snowplow’s validation ensures the data in your warehouse doesn’t require cleansing before analysis

Imagine the possibilities with access to raw data

This raw data can be transformed, aggregated and modeled into any number of interesting reports from fairly standard reporting like sessions to more complex analyses around attribution or engagement. And because the raw data is stored in your warehouse, it can be modeled multiple times to serve multiple teams; one data source of truth addressing multiple use cases.

And there are many more reasons Snowplow Insights is a leading data collection platform for companies that want to do data right:

  • Custom data structures - collect data that is structured to suit your business, don’t be forced into a pre-defined structure
  • Validation - only high-quality, well-structured data makes it through to your warehouse, everything else can be re-processed
  • First-party data - hosted in your infrastructure so browser measures like ITP won’t block your tracking
  • Full ownership - you own your infrastructure, you own your raw data and you own your data model
