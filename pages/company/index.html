Our Mission

We empower Smart People to differentiate with data.

We tech gives companies control of their data, so they can use to drive competitive advantage.

Digital event data is rich, behavioral data.

Today there is very little we don't do on digital platforms. We fall in love on dating services. We wile away our time playing video games. We manage our health through connected devices. We read novels, keep up with the news, order food and book holidays online. We find work through digital platforms and use online services to perform and deliver that work.

We, the data community, have barely scratched the surface of what is possible with that data.

This data is a goldmine. It can be used to answer an impossibly large set of questions. Used right, companies can build a much better understanding of their users. They can identify and seize new opportunities to serve them better. The possibilities are endless.

Take control of your data. Realize the data possibility.

We empower analysts, engineers, product managers, marketers, operations and other specialists to do transformative things with data.

It is hard to use data to differentiate. It requires both creative and technical excellence: creative, because every great piece of insight starts when a Smart Person asks the right question. Using data to answer questions requires technical know how.

We build technology that puts Smart People in control of their data. We cannot build technology to help our users with the creative part of data analysis. But we can build technology that enables them to do what they want with data.

Snowplow How

Ownership

Our technology gives our users ownership of their data. We do not mediate our users access to their own data.

Control.

Our technology gives our users control of their own data. They decide: what data they want to track, what questions they want to ask, how they want to answer those questions, what technology to use and how they want to use the insight gleaned.

Freedom

We set our users free to do what they want with their data.

Yali Sassoon CEO

Yali has spent his whole life working with data in one form or another: to improve operations at AP Moeller Maersk and Vestas Wind Systems, to drive strategic decision making for clients at PwC Strategy and Keplar LLP, and both strategic and operational analytics at OpenX Technologies. Yali is keenly interested in the role data has had to play in the history of reasoning, something he was able to explore studying an MPhil in the History and Philosophy of Science at Cambridge Univversity. He has a BA in Natural Sciences, also from the University of Cambridge.
